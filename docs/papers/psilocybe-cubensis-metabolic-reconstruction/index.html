Computational metabolic reconstruction

Evidence-qualified reconstruction of the Psilocybe cubensis metabolic network from Terpedia

A provenance-linked reaction catalog, defined-medium reachability analysis, and reaction, enzyme, and gene hypotheses.

Published July 19, 2026Terpedia Scientific Study PlatformRevision 1

Companion materials: Full HTML manuscript · Markdown manuscript · Colab notebook · Source network and reports

Abstract

We reconstructed a provenance-linked draft metabolic network for Psilocybe cubensis using UniProt, Rhea, and ChEBI. The catalog contains 826 reactions and 617 unique reactants. Reachability from a glucose-based fungal defined-medium proxy is 1.94% under curated directions and 2.92% under the reversible upper bound. The resulting reaction, enzyme, and gene hypotheses define a transparent curation agenda rather than a claim of complete metabolism.
