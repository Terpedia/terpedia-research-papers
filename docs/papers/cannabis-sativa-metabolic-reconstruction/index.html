Computational metabolic reconstruction

Evidence-qualified reconstruction of the Cannabis sativa metabolic network from Terpedia

A provenance-linked reaction catalog, defined-medium reachability analysis, and evidence-qualified reaction, enzyme, and gene hypotheses.

Published July 19, 2026Terpedia Scientific Study PlatformRevision 1

Companion materials: Markdown manuscript · Colab notebook · Terpedia Knowledge source and data

Abstract

We reconstructed a provenance-linked draft metabolic network for Cannabis sativa in Terpedia and evaluated whether its reaction reactants are reachable from an explicit plant defined-medium proxy. The reference-proteome projection contains 1,086 Rhea master reactions and 831 unique reaction reactants. Sixteen reactants (1.93%) are reachable under curated directions, 24 (2.89%) under a hybrid direction mode, and 26 (3.13%) under a fully reversible structural upper bound. The analysis produces 70 unresolved exact-EC reaction hypotheses, 369 missing-producer hypotheses, and 1,319 blocked-known-reaction hypotheses. This is an auditable research substrate, not a complete Cannabis flux model.
